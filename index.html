Player 1

Rules

  1. Each player rolls a die until either a 1 is rolled or they decide to hold.
  2. If you roll a 1, you lose all points for the round and your turn ends.
  3. If you roll any other number, the total is added to your score for the round.
  4. If you choose to hold instead of roll, your score for the round is added to your total score, and it's the next player's turn.
  5. First player to 100 wins!
